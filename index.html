Mike's Rifts is a specialist retailer of Lake Malawi Cichlids and Lake Tanganyika Cichlids.

All our stocks can be delivered to your door via an overnight courier delivery.

We sell our Lake Malawi Cichlids and Lake Tanganyika Cichlids here in the UK and have been importing and selling these stunning and interesting African cichlid fish since 2009.

Our aim is to source the best quality African rift lake cichlids for our customers backed up with 40 years experience in keeping Malawi and Tanganyika cichlids.

With our links to wild caught exporters in the rift lakes region of East Africa and tank bred African cichlid breeders on the continent we can source many varieties of Rift Valley Cichlid for our customers.

Along with the sourcing and sale of these fish we can advise our customers on which species of Rift Lake Cichlid are compatible and can be housed safely together along with the correct aquarium parameters and setup whether that is for your Lake Malawi cichlid aquarium or your Lake Tanganyika cichlid aquarium.
